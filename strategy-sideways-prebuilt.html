🌐 Iron Condor

Structure: Sell 1 lower strike Put, Buy 1 even lower strike Put + Sell 1 higher strike Call, Buy 1 even higher strike Call

When to use: Range-bound market, low volatility

Max Profit: Net Premium Received

Max Loss: Difference between adjacent strikes - Net Premium Received

🔄 Short Strangle

Structure: Sell 1 OTM Call, Sell 1 OTM Put

When to use: Very low volatility expected, range-bound market

Max Profit: Net Premium Received

Max Loss: Unlimited (on large moves)
